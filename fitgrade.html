THE WHY

WHERE IT STARTED

FitGrade started as a learning project. The goal was simple — build a real SaaS from scratch and learn how the whole thing works end to end. The subject matter chose itself. Fitness was already part of daily life, the gym was already familiar territory, and the trainers there were already doing everything manually — spreadsheets, DMs, handwritten notes.

So the question became: what would it look like if a trainer could manage every athlete from one place? Not just see their progress but actually control access, generate AI training plans on the fly, track injury history, and grade physical readiness the way a coach grades film?

That question turned into months of iteration. Meeting with trainers at the gym. Rebuilding screens from scratch when they didn't feel right. Pushing builds to TestFlight and watching real people use it. FitGrade is what happened when learning to build met something worth building.

"I wanted to learn how to build a SaaS so I picked an app I could use every day. I met with trainers at the gym and worked until it was finished."

DEPLOYEAS Build / Submit
01
ROLE-BASED ACCESS
Four distinct roles — Trainer, Athlete, Coach, and Parent — each with their own tailored dashboard, permissions, and data visibility. A trainer can see everything. An athlete sees only their own plan.
02
3D BODY HOLOGRAM
SVG-rendered body map that grades each muscle group based on training data. Visual health grading that tells athletes exactly where they're strong and where they need work.
03
AI PLAN GENERATION
Claude API integration generates personalized 7-day training programs based on sport, position, injury history, fitness level, and coach preferences. Generated in seconds.
04
6-DIGIT JOIN CODES
Athletes join a trainer's roster by entering a unique 6-digit code. No manual linking, no admin overhead. One code, one tap, instant roster connection.
05
AUDIT LOG
Every action is timestamped and logged — plan changes, clearance decisions, session completions. Full accountability for coaches managing multiple athletes.
06
CLEARANCE WORKFLOW
Injury tracking with a formal clearance system. Trainers flag athletes as injured, document the condition, and formally clear them before they return to full training.
THE HARD PARTS

WHAT PUSHED BACK

01
EAS BUILD ENVIRONMENT INJECTION
The App Store rejection at build 2.1.0 came down to a missing "environment": "production" flag in the EAS production build profile. Without it, the API key injection wasn't firing and the Claude API calls were failing silently. Traced it down line by line.
02
ROLE-BASED DATA ARCHITECTURE
Designing a Firestore structure that could serve four different roles from the same database without leaking data across permission levels. Every query had to be scoped to the authenticated user's role before returning results.
03
SVG BODY HOLOGRAM
Building a body map that updates in real time based on training data meant mapping every muscle group to an SVG path, binding each path to a color scale, and making the whole thing interactive without killing performance on device.
04
REAL TRAINER FEEDBACK LOOPS
The hardest challenge wasn't technical — it was going back to the gym after a build and hearing that something didn't work the way a real trainer expected. Several full UI rebuilds came from those conversations.
DESIGN PROCESS

HOW IT WAS BUILT

PHASE 01
RESEARCH
Talked to trainers at the gym directly. Understood their workflow, what they hated about existing tools, and what they actually needed day to day. No assumptions.
PHASE 02
ARCHITECTURE
Mapped out the role system, data model, and navigation structure before writing a single component. Four roles, one database, zero data leakage.
PHASE 03
ITERATION
Multiple full UI rebuilds. The hologram went through three versions. The join code system replaced a more complex invite flow that was too clunky to use in a gym setting.
PHASE 04
TESTING
TestFlight builds pushed to real trainers and athletes. Feedback collected in the gym, not on a form. Real usage surfaced things no simulator ever would.

THE ANGLE

WHAT MAKES IT DIFFERENT

THE THING NO ONE ELSE HAS
A BODY THAT GRADES ITSELF
Every fitness app shows you numbers. FitGrade shows you a body — your body — color-graded by muscle group based on actual training data. It's the difference between reading a spreadsheet and reading a health scan.
THE BUSINESS MODEL INSIGHT
BUILT FOR THE TRAINER, NOT THE ATHLETE
Every other fitness app goes direct to consumer. FitGrade goes B2B — the trainer is the customer, the athlete is the user. That means stickier retention, higher value, and a model that scales with a gym's client roster.
THE AI LAYER
PLANS IN SECONDS, NOT HOURS
A trainer writing a custom 7-day program for every athlete is hours of work. FitGrade does it in seconds via Claude API, then lets the trainer edit, approve, or regenerate. AI does the draft, the trainer does the judgment.
THE INTEGRITY LAYER
EVERY ACTION LOGGED
When an athlete gets injured and then cleared to return, that decision is documented. The audit log makes FitGrade the only fitness platform where coaches can actually prove their duty of care.

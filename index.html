ABOUT

THE ARCHITECT

Brandon Montford is a 21-year-old mobile engineer from Atlanta, GA with a passion for building apps that make a real difference. Recent graduate of Concordia University Chicago with a dual Bachelor's in Computer Science and Communications. Dean's List, Best Capstone Award, top 10 nationally in an IEEE competition, and inaugural Honor Council member.

Former Division III student-athlete in football and track. That same discipline carries into every build.

The focus is on apps that actually help people. Assistive technology, health tools, speech assistance, autonomous AI agents, and games. VoiceBridge was built for a family member. Miracle League grew from years of volunteering with an adaptive baseball league. The range is the point.
